Our Story

Founded in 2019, ECOMIXX has a continuing commitment to support local and national socio-economic development. Our quarry and crusher plants were established to provide high-quality aggregates for various land development and construction needs. To ensure consistent and reliable supply, specialized heavy equipment is used together with state-of-the-art transport and communication facilities to make sure the quality of production and on-time delivery is always guaranteed. We will not waiver in our promise to provide meaningful employment to our associates and maintain the ideal balance of a sustainable environment while fulfilling our contribution to nation-building.

Ecomixx Concrete Solution Incorporated has an extensive selection of high-quality sand and gravel and can provide delivery services to ensure your project is completed on time and on budget. We also offer helpful advice and tips to make sure you get the most out of your purchase. Our team is friendly, knowledgeable, and always happy to help. Please don't hesitate to contact us with any questions or concerns you may have. We look forward to helping you get the best results for your project.
